Mailing List

The main point of contact for the GPE project is the mailing list gpe@handhelds.org.

To subscribe to the list go to http://handhelds.org/mailman/listinfo/gpe and follow the instructions.

You can read past articles in the mailing list archive at http://handhelds.org/pipermail/gpe/.

IRC

GPE coders and users can be be found on the #gpe channel of irc.handhelds.org (port 6667).

GPE around the globe

GPE team members are spread all around the globe. If you're a GPE user/developer/hacker and are headed somewhere, check here to see if there's anyone you can visit!

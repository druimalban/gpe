Some nice games against boring meetings would be nice, like Tetris, Mines, etc.

Philip Blundell (philb@gnu.org) posted the following to the GPE mailinglist:

Obviously, no PDA will have any credibility on the street unless it includes Tetris and Solitaire. So, I tracked down an implementation of each, bashed them about a bit to make them usable on the iPAQ, and here they are:

ftp://ftp.armlinux.org/users/philb/gtktetris (iPAQ binary)
ftp://ftp.armlinux.org/users/philb/gtktetris-0.5-pb.tar.gz (source)
ftp://ftp.armlinux.org/users/philb/sol (iPAQ binary)
ftp://ftp.armlinux.org/users/philb/sol.tar.gz (source)

The Solitaire one could use some more work: most of the binary size and startup time is due to the way it stores the card pixmaps, and the images themselves are a bit fuzzier than would be ideal.
